What is Social Media Management?

Social media management involves creating, scheduling, analyzing, and engaging with content posted on social media platforms to build a brand's online presence and foster community engagement.


Our course equips you with the skills to effectively manage social media accounts, develop strategic campaigns, and analyze performance metrics to drive success.


Who is this course designed for?

This course is designed for aspiring social media managers, marketing professionals, entrepreneurs, and anyone looking to enhance their skills in managing social media platforms to effectively build and engage with online communities.

Your Learning Journey

Week 1 - Onboarding, Introduction, Fundamentals of Social Media Management

The first week introduces students to the course structure, covers the fundamentals of social media management, and sets up the foundational knowledge needed for the rest of the course.

Week 2 & 3 - Creating Social Media Strategy Template and Developing Content Plans

Weeks two and three focus on creating a comprehensive social media strategy template and developing effective content plans for various platforms.

Week 4 - Crafting a Social Media Advertising Plan and Understanding Platform Algorithms

The fourth week teaches students how to craft a social media advertising plan and provides an in-depth understanding of platform algorithms and how they affect content visibility.

Week 5 & 6- Social Media Analytics, Automation, and Creating Highly Engaging Content

Weeks five and six delve into social media analytics and automation, and guide students on creating highly engaging and optimized content.

Week 7 - Applying Insights from Social Media Analytics to Improve Engagement

The seventh week focuses on applying insights from social media analytics to improve engagement and campaign performance.

Week 8 - Leveraging Influencer Partnerships and Community Management

In the eighth week, students learn how to leverage influencer partnerships and effectively manage online communities to drive growth and engagement.

Week 9 & 10 - Advanced Techniques and Social Media Campaign Management

Weeks nine and ten cover advanced social media management techniques and best practices for managing campaigns effectively across multiple platforms.

Week 11 & 12 - Capstone Project Completion and Presentation

Weeks eleven and twelve are dedicated to the completion of a capstone project, where students apply their accumulated knowledge to a comprehensive social media campaign, culminating in a final presentation.

Week 13 - Graduation

The final week involves a review of the course content, celebration of achievements, discussion of next steps and career opportunities in social media management, and the distribution of certificates of completion along with final feedback.
