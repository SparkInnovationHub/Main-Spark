What is Data Analysis?

Data analysis is a crucial practice found across various industries, both in digital environments and in traditional business settings. It involves the systematic evaluation of data to extract meaningful insights, using statistical and computational techniques to inform decision-making.


As the importance of data-driven decisions grows, so does the cultural and strategic significance of data analysis. This course will teach you the fundamental principles of data analysis through practical examples, along with advanced tips and techniques to help you become a proficient data analyst.


Who is this course designed for?

This course is ideal for beginners in data analysis, as well as those with a basic understanding looking to refine and expand their analytical skills.

Your Learning Journey

Week 1 & 2 - Onboarding, Introduction to Data Analysis, and Fundamentals of Excel

Course onboarding, introduction to data analysis, and an overview of key concepts. Learn the fundamentals of Excel, including data entry, formatting, and basic functions.

Week 3 - Data Cleaning and Manipulation in Excel

Introduction to data cleaning techniques in Excel. Learn how to manipulate data using functions like VLOOKUP, Pivot Tables, and basic formulas.

Week 4 - Basic Data Visualization with Excel

Understanding charts and graphs in Excel. Learn to create and interpret basic visualizations like bar charts, line charts, and pie charts.

Week 5 - Introduction to Descriptive Statistics in Excel

Learn the basics of descriptive statistics, including mean, median, mode, and standard deviation. Perform basic statistical analysis using Excel functions.

Week 6 - Introduction to Power BI

Get started with Power BI, understanding its interface and basic functionalities. Learn to connect Power BI to Excel data sources and create simple visualizations.

Week 7 - Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) Basics in Power BI

Introduction to DAX, the formula language of Power BI. Learn to create calculated columns, measures, and simple DAX expressions.

Week 8 & 9 - Data Analysis and Reporting in Power BI

Learn to create comprehensive reports in Power BI. Understand how to design dashboards and share reports with others.

Week 10 - Introduction to Google Sheets for Data Analysis

Learn basic data analysis techniques using Google Sheets. Explore Google Sheets functions, conditional formatting, and simple data visualization.

Week 11 & 12 - Capstone Project (Basic Data Analysis)

Apply your skills to a real-world data analysis project. Work on cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing a dataset, then present your findings.

Week 13 - Graduation

The final week involves reviewing the course content, celebrating achievements, discussing next steps and career opportunities in web development, and receiving certificates of completion and final feedback.
