About the program

Project 1000 - Innovate Benue is an initiative to provide free tech training to 1,000 individuals across Benue State. This program is tailored for beginners and aspiring innovators who are ready to learn and grow in the tech space.

Our mission is to build a tech-ecosystem in Benue by offering hands-on training in web development, graphic design, and other in-demand tech areas.

Admission Requirement

To qualify for this free training, all you need is:

  • A laptop
  • A passion to learn and grow in tech!
